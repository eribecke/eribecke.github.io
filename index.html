Gameplay Summary

Fox Flight is a 2D sidescrolling game where your goal is to fly Jeffry, the berry-loving fox, to the berry meadows. Navigate your way through the skies and earn an upgrade for Jeffry's plane. Watch out for the angry blue birds!
